🐾 Plan B

PetConnect
Discovery Grid

Grid-based pet discovery experience — replacing Tinder-style swipe cards with a Grindr-style two-column browsing grid.

Version 2.0 July 2026 Design Spec — Not Code
01 Design Direction
Pet Discovery, Not Dating
The core shift: from one-at-a-time swiping to browsing many pets at once. The design must feel like a trusted pet community platform — warm, clean, and trustworthy — never like a human dating app.
What we're keeping
Existing branding and logo identity
Navigation structure (4 tabs)
Pet profile screen (redesigned CTA)
Favorites, hide, report, block actions
Arabic RTL as primary language
What's changing
Swipe cards → Two-column grid
Like/Pass → "Send Interest" button
Instant chat → Chat after owner accepts
Unlimited actions → 20 free interests
No filters → Chip bar + filter sheet

Palette
